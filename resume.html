CLIFFORD BRYCELEA

1721 Montano Street
Santa Fe, New Mexico 87501
(505) 984-8632
Professional Artist Painter

General Background:

Professional painter since 1976. Specializing in watercolor, acrylic and pen/ink mystical and scenery. Work related to the Native American theme. A member of the Indian Arts and Crafts Association (LACA), 1987 Artist of the Year Award and the Southwestern Association for Indian Arts, Inc (SWALA).

Experience:

1984 - Graphic work. Mural and logo design. Painted in High School Gymnasium, Dulce, New Mexico.
1974 - Mural Paintings at Interculteral Center and Kiva Room, Fort Lewis College, Durango, Colorado.

Publications:

1997 - DINE BIKAYAH (Navajo Lands) Map; Published by Time Traveler Maps, Mancos, Colorado.
1995 - FIRE IN THE MIND by George Johnson, Borzoi Book Published by Alfred A. Knopf, Inc., Cover design.
1995 - MOON AND OTTER AND FROG by Laurie Simms, published by Hyperion Books for Children, New York. Illustration and cover design.
1994 - THE TALKING WIND by Jack Hawkins, published by Ute Mountain Publishing, cover design.
1992 - June issue of SOUTHWEST ART MAGAZINE. Cover and interviewed article.
1991 - SANTA FE CHAMBER OF COMMERCE MAGAZINE cover design.
1987 - HAUNTED MESA by Louis LAmour. Bantam Books, New York. Illustration and cover design.
1984 - PIECES OF WHITE SHELL by Terry Tempest Williams. Charles Scribners Sons, New York. Illustration and cover.
1979 - Title VII classroom publication. Dulce Elementary School. Illustration.
1976 - AMERICAN WAY MAGAZINE by American Airlines. Cover and illustrated short story by Louis LAmour.

Video Publications:

1997 - "Dancing with Photons" video by Beverly Morris and KNME-TV, Chain Reaction Production, Santa Fe, New Mexico.
1996 - "Sky Watchers" video by DISCOVER MAGAZINE, Boston, Massachusetts.

Education:

Fort Lewis College, Durango, Colorado.

Attended 1972 through 1975, BA Degree in Art.
